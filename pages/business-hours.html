Hours

The RTP Errand Service will accept orders any day of the week, Sunday through Saturday. We also offer deliveries throughout the greater Raleigh-Durham area all week, though our standard delivery hours vary. If you have a need for an immediate delivery, or a delivery that is significantly outside of our typical delivery windows, just let us know and we will see if we can work something out.

Orders Accepted every day from 8:30am - 10:00 pm
Deliveries Mon-Fri from 4:00 pm - 8:00 pm
Deliveries Weekends from 3:00 pm - 7:00 pm
