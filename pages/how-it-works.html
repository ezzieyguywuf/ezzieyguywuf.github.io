How It Works

Using our service is as easy as 1, 2, 3

  1. Call, text, or email us with your request
  2. Either prepay via paypal, Square, Google Wallet (or any other service you prefer) or provide us with a valid Credit Card number to keep on file. This is so we can pay for your groceries, dry cleaning, etc. You will not be charged for the delivery fee until after delivery.
  3. Confirm that our estimated delivery time will work for you

That's it!
